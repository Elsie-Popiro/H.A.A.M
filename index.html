ABOUT US

Her Awareness of Menstrual Matters (HAAM) is a franchise offering menstrual health education and reusable pads.
It trains schoolgirls to become menstrual coaches who educate their peers. Schools, counsils, refugee camps and communities pay for this training.
The reusable pads are cost-effective and produced in bulk. Revenue comes from sales to governments, NGOs (e.g WHO, UNICEF, UN WOMEN) and
local retailers such as small shops in rural areas.
